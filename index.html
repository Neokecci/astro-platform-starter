About Us

Neokecci Multiconcepts Company Limited is a Nigerian-based company founded in 2005 and incorporated in 2010 (RC 926675). We specialize in sustainable development, capacity building, and enterprise support across the Niger Delta region. We assist multinational organizations in implementing community development projects, impact assessments, and youth and women entrepreneurship programmes.

We are accredited Shell LiveWIRE trainers, having trained and mentored over 1,700 entrepreneurs and coordinated over 242 rural development projects valued at over $10 million across 18 Niger Delta communities. Our expertise also includes Monitoring and Evaluation (M&E), capacity building, impact assessment, and business coaching.
