VIBES — Powered by NLNG

The Vocational, Innovation, Business, and Empowerment Scheme (VIBES) is a flagship programme powered by NLNG and implemented by Neokecci. It delivers high-impact business training, mentorship, networking opportunities, and—best of all—access to grants and MSME loans for aspiring and existing entrepreneurs in NLNG’s 110 host communities.

Participants benefit from expert-led sessions, real-world business insights, and long-term support. Join the next cohort and take your business to the next level.
